What I've Been:

Hi, I'm Peyton, a musician and developer.

I started my band Skating Polly with my sister Kelli back in 2009. Now we tour all over the world and write as a trio with our brother Kurtis.

When I’m not on the road, you can find me at home with my chihuahua Georgie. We love having cozy days in, watching movies and working on art but we're also always up for a long day out on the trails with our binoculars (although Georgie prefers I leave the binos in the car)!

Much to Georgie's dismay, I spend a lot of my downtime coding. Lately, I’ve been working on an online platrformer game to go alongside my band's album Chaos County Line, expanding my bird identification game, Bird Match, and building out a fun and interactive user profile system for Skating Polly's website.

And, when I need a break from touring or coding, I'm usually gaming, birdwatching, or curled up with a book or a movie and Georgie.
